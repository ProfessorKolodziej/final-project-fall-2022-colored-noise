Pink Noise

Say hello to pink noise, the dreamy lullaby for your sleep sanctuary! It is a type of sound that's characterized by equal energy per octave, which means it has more power in the lower frequencies compared to higher ones.As a rhythmic blend of frequencies with a special touch, it's like a sonic sandman, slowing brain waves and promoting a harmonious slumber. With its power-packed lower frequencies, pink noise serenades you into dreamland, masking disturbances like a sleep-savvy ninja. So, if you're on a quest for the ultimate beauty sleep, let pink noise be your nightly companion.
