Blue Noise

Blue noise, scientifically characterized by a higher energy concentration in higher frequencies, is like the refreshing splash of waves against the shore. Imagine it as a sonic breeze with more emphasis on the higher notes, creating a crisp and invigorating auditory experience. Blue noise plays a noteworthy role in alleviating tinnitus. By masking or distracting from the persistent ringing or buzzing sounds associated with tinnitus, blue noise offers relief and a reprieve for those experiencing this auditory condition. It's like a musical antidote, providing a harmonious escape from the monotony of tinnitus-related sounds.
